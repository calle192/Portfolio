Two Reasons for Sequences of Images

Cycling through images can be used for various reasons. One reason I could think of is a game for kids. You could make a game where little kids could match an image to another image. You could make a game where they have to make a picture with a series of images like a puzzle. Or perhaps a game where the image they have to select is the answer to a question. Another reason for images that cycle with a button would be a site that sells something. A good example is amazon. When you look at an item to buy it often comes with multiple pictures that you cycle through with a click of a button.
